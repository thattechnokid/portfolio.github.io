My Story


Hello, and my name is Cristian Mendoza! Welcome again to my portfolio website! Before we get into my experiences and what not, I am going to give you a little background to how I got to where I am. So sit down, grab some popcorn...or not, and get ready to read my *cough* "Interesting" story.

It all started about 6 years ago, when I was in 8th grade, and I had a pretty huge interest in computers. So I was like, "Okay... wouldn't it be cool if I could create my own website?" So I found a drag-and-drop program called Webs, and made my first site for my Chemistry class. After doing that, my teachers wanted me to take a computer class that taught me the BASICS of HTML and CSS. So I started self-teaching myself web design basics, and then made more websites through Weebly, while still tweaking them with my knowledge. Now fast-forward about 4 years, and to me, the whole drag-and-drop business started to get pretty dull and boring. Now I wanted to start building things from scratch. But I was working 24/7/365 in college at California Baptist University. There I was planning on majoring in Software Engineering. So after my first year, I asked myself, "What would you rather do? Spend another 3 years there, and be over $100,000 in debt, or stay home, and make money now to be able to get enough money to start a family?" So naturally I chose the latter of the two.



That was when I was told about a fantastic organization called Geekwise Academy, which is an amazing place where you get to know new people and learn some amazing skillsets. Here, I was able to learn a lot of new things, including how to make a website from scratch!
